# Mind Over Matter - Cataclysm BN Port

Psionic powers mod ported from CDDA for Cataclysm Bright Nights.

## Features

- **9 Psionic Schools**:
  - Telekinesis - Manipulate objects with your mind
  - Biokinesis - Enhance and control your body
  - Telepathy - Read and influence minds
  - Pyrokinesis - Manipulate heat and fire
  - Electrokinesis - Channel electricity
  - Photokinesis - Control light
  - Clairsentience - Enhanced senses and precognition
  - Teleportation - Warp space
  - Vitakinesis - Manipulate life energy

- **Psychic Knacks** - Passive abilities for each school

## Installation

Drop this folder into your `data/mods/` directory.

## Usage

1. Enable the mod in game
2. Seek "Awakening" to unlock your first powers
3. Learn powers through recipes and practice
